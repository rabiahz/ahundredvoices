A Hundred Voices
Though I am working but still I don't have the independence
that is required for a person to freely move. I have
to ask for permission for mostly everything and can't
do things on my own. People take advantage of my simplicity
at workplace as I can not say no to work and for that matter
I am overloaded with work or given a task which I am not
supposed to do but I still do it. Forced to believe that a woman
is always wrong may that be driving a car on the road
or looking after your kids or even cooking in your own kitchen...
a man can never be wrong and that is drilled into our
minds so eventually one starts taking the blame for every
wrong doing that happens in ones life.
