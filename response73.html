A Hundred Voices






So many of men from my extended family don't want
to see me work and earning. And used to question
my mother for that. "Why is she earning; are you using
her money in your expenses?" They even tried to stop
my education but my mom was a great support.
Even shamed my father saying "Beti k pese khata hai"
My father is a retired officer. He worked throughout
his life just for us.
